drg. Miracle Sitompul

The woman who is truly a Miracle

Miracle Sitompul or familiarly called Mira is a woman from Jambi who has successfully inspiring many people. Through her social media accounts such as Instagram, Tiktok, and YouTube, Mira often shares educational content about her background as a dentist and tips on answering college entrance examination (SNBT) questions. She also often posts her life journey to motivate her followers.

Here's The Miracle's Life Journey:

  • 1999 - Born in Tanjung Pinang, Riau and raised in Jambi
  • 2014 - Takes senior high school (SHS) at SMA Negeri 1 Jambi
  • 2017 - Moves to Jakarta and leaves her family to pursue her study as a student of the Faculty of Dentistry at the University of Indonesia.
  • 2019 - During college, Mira takes part in the Abang None of Central Jakarta audition and becomes the First Runner-up None of Central Jakarta 2019.
  • 2021 - Graduates in 3.5 years with cumlaude predicate, continues as a dental co-assistant, and finishes it in 2023.
  • 2021 - Has a hobby of solving math problems encourages Mira to be part of Zenius (E-Learning Providers) as a Zencore Content Developer for a year and becomes brand ambassador of Ajaib.
  • 2024 - A year after completing her studies, Mira decides to participate in Puteri Indonesia 2024 beauty pageant. Her application at the beginning of the year leads to her becoming Puteri Indonesia Jambi, included in the Top 16 Puteri Indonesia, and being crowned as Puteri Intelegensia III.
  • With the award, Mira dedicates herself to participating in Puteri Indonesia activities, being a speaker at seminars conducted in Jakarta, and spreading benefits through her content. Since November, Mira has officially moved to Dompu, West Nusa Tenggara to continue her intership as a dentist at the local health center with her fellow dentists.
